What is the EDGE App?

The EDGE app was a prototype application I built for students in EDGE to schedule their classes and find information about intensives and workshops. I was never able to complete it, due to my lack of knowledge of xCode and my beginner knowledge of design, but it helped me learn a lot more about design and developing for an audience.
