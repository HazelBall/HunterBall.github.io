What is Fresh.CSS

Fresh.CSS is a lightweight CSS framework, for small projects and portfolio pages. With only 384 Lines of Code, it is small, but powerful, allowing developers to easily style basic elements of a website.


Unlike most lightweight frameworks, Fresh.CSS will come in different editions. Currently, there are two for Fresh.CSS, Fresh Lemon and Fresh Lime. Fresh-Lemon.CSS is the basic features, and Fresh-Lime.CSS adds in an array of colors
