NASA
Job Shadow

During the week of April 30th - May 4th, I shadowed an employee at the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center in Maryland. This shadow showed me the opportunities and jobs at NASA. As a kid, the only jobs you think of with NASA are Astronauts, Engineers, and Mission Control, but there are so many other jobs to make the facilities and organization work. I also learned a lot about internship possibilities, and I will be applying for an internship during the summer between my Junior and Senior year.

One of the most intriguing aspects of NASA GSFC was that it was very similar to a College campus. There are clubs, activities, events, and groups. There are all kinds of things to do at NASA. At one point, I saw the Karate Club while leaving one of the buildings, practicing their skills in the hallway. There is a huge community feel, and eveyone at NASA has to work together, even without knowing everyone (there are 10,000 people at GSFC alone), to keep the organization moving forward.

After going to NASA, I believe it is definitely a possible career option, and because I now have contacts at NASA, I believe it would be an amazing opportunity for internships and maybe even jobs.
