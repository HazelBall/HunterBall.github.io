FBI Agent Brutally Murdered In House, No Suspect Found.

FBI is still looking for the murderer who took Scott Leverage's life.


Last wednesday, the life of Scott Leverage was taken. A FBI agent for over 20 years and a husband and father of one, FBI is still unable to find any suspects.


Scott was found inside his trash can. There were several missing body parts, and several gash wounds from a knife, and gunshots. Although everything for a crime scene was carried out correctly, FBI could find no evidence of any possible suspect at the crime scene.


Many people point to his cousin, Henry, or his son, David, as Scott had a large sum of money from his career, and they could have wanted the money. However, the FBI and police declined to comment about possible suspects.


It is rumored the FBI is looking into a possible dark web hacktivist as the mastermind behind the crime, but again, no comments were given.
